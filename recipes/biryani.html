Biryani

THis is the famouse hyderabad Dhum biryani, and below is the way you can prepare it

Place black peppercorns, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and kala jeera in a spice grinder; grind into a fine powder.

Ingredients

  • cloves
  • basmathi rice
  • cardamon
  • cilantro leaves
  • yogurt
  • chicken

Steps

  1. Place black peppercorns, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and kala jeera in a spice grinder; grind into a fine powder.
  2. Place cilantro and mint leaves in a food processor; pulse until coarsely chopped.
